BUDGET-FRIENDLY RESTAURANTS

Hawker Chan

The local branch of the famous Singaporean eatery known for its Michelin-starred street food. Specializing in delectable soy sauce chicken, rice, and noodle dishes.

What to order: Wonton Soup (P109), Soya Sauce Chicken Rice (Php 168)

Ono Poke

A restaurant that specializes in serving Hawaiian poke bowls. Known for its fresh and healthy ingredients, it offers customizable bowls featuring a variety of proteins, toppings, and sauces.

What to order: Spam Musubi Bowl (P240), Tuna Poke Bowl (P275), Crazy Kani Bowl (P175)

Recovery Food

A casual dining restaurant known for its comforting and hearty Filipino dishes. It includes favorites such as tapa, arroz caldo, and other rice meals.

What to order: Happy Beef Rice (P175), Tapa de Morning (P195), S.S.T. (P195)

Khao Khai Thai Chicken House

A restaurant that specializes in authentic Thai street food, particularly focusing on Thai-style fried and grilled chicken.

What to order: Gai Tod 1 PC. (P128), Fried Chicken Krapao (P225), Kor Moo Yang (P148)

Oh My Greek

Offering a taste of authentic Greek cuisine. Known for its fresh and flavorful dishes, the menu features a variety of Greek staples such as gyros, souvlaki, and moussaka.

What to order: Chicken or Pork Gyros Wrap Regular (P169), Pork Gyros with Greek Rice (P265)

Go Bento

A popular quick-service restaurant chain specializing in Japanese-style bento meals.

What to order: Yakitori Bento Box (P180), Gyudon (P155), Pork Katsu Curry Bento Box (P190), Chicken Teriyaki Don (P155)

Medley Modern Mediterranean

A restaurant known for its contemporary approach to Mediterranean cuisine. Offering a diverse menu inspired by flavors from countries such as Greece, Turkey, and Lebanon.

What to order: Falafel Crunch Rice Bowl (P279), Levantine Chicken Rice Bowl (P289), Beef Kofte Pita (P229)

Pancake House

It is famous for its wide array of pancakes, including classic buttermilk pancakes served with various toppings such as butter and syrup, as well as other breakfast items like waffles, omelets, and hearty Filipino breakfast sets.

What to order: Original Pan Chicken Burger Regular (P179), Golden Brown Waffles (P208)
